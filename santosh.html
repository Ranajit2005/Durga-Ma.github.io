Santosh Mitra Square Durga Puja or Lebutala park

Natabar Dutta Row Road Lebutala Bowbazar, Kolkata- 700014

In the Bengali year 1343 and the English year 1936, a group of people of the Sealdah thought of worshipping Barowari Durga Pujo on a small area of Chatterjee’s in Serpentine Lane. So they established a Barowari Durga puja now, which you should include in the Kolkata puja tour. Each year, they try something new and have been successful in it. It’s one of the best pandals of Durga Puja in North Kolkata.

Landmark – Near Muchipara Police Station

Theme-This year puja committee chose the “Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav” as their pandal theme and the visitors can see the India Gate, Red Fort and Parliament House in their pandal theme.

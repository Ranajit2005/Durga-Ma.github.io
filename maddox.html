Maddox Square Durga puja

Earle St and Pankaj Mullick Sarani, Garcha, Ballygunge, Kolkata-700025, WB, India.

Durga Puja, hosted by Maddox Park, is famous in Kolkata. The park is a frequent visiting place for every group of people. Young ones go there to play games like cricket, soccer, etc. The elderly visit the park to take a walk or do yoga. We know Maddox Square as “Thek” (Adda) locally.

Landmark – Ritchie Road.
Theme- Traditional

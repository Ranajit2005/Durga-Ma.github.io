Singhi Park Sarbojonin Durgotsav

The puja is organized by the Singhi Park Sarbojanin Durgotsav committee every year, and it’s the most prestigious puja in Bengal. The club donated to Bharat Sevashram & C M Relief Fund during the Amphan. They distributed clothes and food during the lockdown. Singhi Park is famous for organizing blood donations and health checkup camps.

Landmark – Gariahat Police Station.

Theme - Comic characters by Narayan Debnath

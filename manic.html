Manicktala Chaltabagan Lohapatty Durga Puja

77, Raja Ram Mohan Roy Sarani Kolkata- 700009

In 1943, Maniktala Chattabganj Lohapati Durgapuja was founded. This Barwari Puja is founded by Chaltabagan club President Lucky Moon Jaiswal (87 years old). The club builds a magnificent puja mandap with some new themes every year.

Landmark – Opposite Manicktala Ganguram streets.

Theme- ‘Primeval Architecture' of Chola period

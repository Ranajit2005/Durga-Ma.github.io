Md. Ali Park (Youth Association)

41A, TARA CHAND DUTTA STREET KOLKATA – 700073

MD ALI PARK Durga Puja is also known as Youth Association Puja. It’s one of the most prestigious clubs in North Kolkata, established in 1969 at 39 Tara Chand Dutta Street. This puja started because the Famous Fire Brigade Puja was closed in 1968.

66 Pally Sarbojanin Durgotsab Committee Puja Pandal

Every year, the 66 Pally Sarbojanin Durgotsab Puja Committee sets up an impressive pandal and hosts a grand Durga Puja celebration. This puja was started in the year 1951. Every year this club has done unique things. Last year four female priests worshipped Debi Durga. The puja committee has also arranged for several cultural programs to be held during the festival, including music performances and dance recitals.
